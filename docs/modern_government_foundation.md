# EU4 2K Modern Government Foundation

## Audit result

Extended Timeline already models modern politics through later-tier reforms such as parliamentary government, presidential government, multi-party systems, authoritarian rule, military government and communist government. However, countries continue to inherit medieval or early-modern base reforms underneath those layers, and several modern reform names referenced by ET history are not actual reform definitions.

The safest correction is to retain ET's political layers while replacing the base reform after 1836 with a modern constitutional template.

## Implemented templates

| Government | Template | Core behavior |
|---|---|---|
| Monarchy | Parliamentary Monarchy | Lower absolutism, stability and diplomatic legitimacy |
| Monarchy | Modern Absolute Monarchy | Central authority, autonomy reduction and stability |
| Republic | Parliamentary Republic | Four-year elections, republican tradition and diplomacy |
| Republic | Presidential Republic | Four-year elections, administration and taxation |
| Republic | Authoritarian Republic | No term election, manpower and internal control |
| Republic | Socialist State | No term election, production, force limit and internal control |

All templates use standard EU4 mechanics and existing icons. They do not require a DLC-specific government ability.

## Startup normalization

For campaigns beginning in or after 1836:

- Monarchies with parliamentary or multi-party reforms become Parliamentary Monarchies.
- Other monarchies become Modern Absolute Monarchies.
- Republics with communist government become Socialist States.
- Republics with authoritarian, dictatorial, single-party, military, fascist or presidential-despot reforms become Authoritarian Republics.
- Republics with parliamentary reform become Parliamentary Republics.
- Other republics become Presidential Republics.

This preserves ET's dated political history and later reform layers while removing generic feudal, oligarchic and other pre-modern base forms once play begins.

Theocracies and tribal governments are deliberately unchanged. The Holy See and comparable religious states need separate treatment, while remaining tribal governments require country-specific review rather than automatic conversion.

## Validation

Test the 1836 and 2000 bookmarks and inspect representative countries from every category. Confirm elections, heirs, legitimacy or republican tradition, estates, reform progress and ruler succession remain functional. Organization mechanics and national ideas remain outside this milestone.
