# EU4 2K

Private Extended Timeline 1.18.2 fork for Europa Universalis IV 1.37.

- Campaign dates: `1836.1.1` through `9999.12.31`
- Complete ET map, history, countries, technology, events and mechanics
- ET secularism and irreligious religion mechanics removed
- Affected history assignments retain their nearest conventional religion
- Technology levels 78–84 reworked for a coherent 1835–1945 progression
- Technology levels 85–95 reworked for a coherent 1960–2100 progression
- Technology years synchronized through 2100
- Modern technology groups initialize at level 78 in the 1836 scenario
- Generated future technology levels continue every 50 years through 9950
- Modern fortification chain and rebalanced industrial energy buildings
- Building interfaces display Fort 18th and all four modern fort tiers
- Modern fort tiers include localized titles, descriptions and reused fort art
- Non-stacking transport chain extends through post-2100 infrastructure

Enable only EU4 2K when testing. Changes should be tested in a new campaign;
existing saves can retain removed religion IDs.
